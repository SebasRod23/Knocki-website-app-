Presenting: Knocki

Meet Knocki

A small wireless device that instantly transforms ordinary surfaces (walls, tables, doors, furniture, countertops, & more) into powerful yet easy to access remote controls for the smart technology around you.


BRING YOUR SURFACES TO LIFE.

Some functions...

Turn your lights on/off

Find your lost cellphone

Climate control with a tap

Know if a door is knocked

Set or snooze an alarm

Brew coffee from your bed

Receive an alert when mail arrives

Tap into your favorite tunes

Set the mood with a gesture

Text when leaving or arriving

And so many more integrations
