The "Think aloud" Evaluation technique

There were 4 different people interacting with our application "Knocki" to obtain information about the evaluation technique "Think Aloud" after conducting the interviews we managed to highlight the following points:


POSITIVES:

  • The design of the main screen is simple and it is possible to understand well that the logo and slogan design are pleasant to the user
  • They consider that it is very easy to create an account because it asks for very little data, there are applications that ask for many and more tedious
  • They like the management design of the devices, how the devices are divided by room and consider that it is easy to add new commands
  • They consider that people of older age people who are not as very associated with technology can easily understand the application
  • They consider the application quite interesting since the amount that can be done with it are quite broad

NEGATIVES:

  • The buttons are quite small, which makes navigation difficult through the app.
  • They consider it a very expensive device, since you would need one in each room and that would be a great investment
  • A user found that the device does not have a great differentiator from others already available in the market, such as Alexa by Amazon which, for him, finds much more practical, since only one device is used and only with the voice you can perform all the commands that you want, in the part of the house that you are

CHANGES:

  • They consider that it is easier, if from the cell phone you could perform all the actions of the different app without having to open each one
  • A user highlighted that if you do not have an account and you are not registered this should appear larger so that new users are motivated to create an account, and in turn, the login button should be the button small
  • Change in the size of the buttons
