Low-level Prototype

Landing Page

In this first window we have two buttons so the user decides to either go to the Log In page or the Create an Account page.

Login Page

This is the Log In Page, in this you will need to have an active email and the correct password, you have a button in case you chose Log in and you didn’t have an account

Create Account Page

This is the create an account where we just ask your name and the email you are going to activate, and the password you choose. There’s also an extra button in case you already had an account

User's Knockis Page

This is the main page, in here you will be able to see which knockis are the ones you have registered. Also, in the plus button you will be able to add new knockis, and in the hamburguer menu you will be able to see your profile and other options

Menu

This is how the menu looks broken down. If you click on one of the surfaces you will be able to add more gestures, the gestures activate sounds or lights or other things.

Add-Knocki Page

This is the window where you add a knocki, in here you just choose the room where is located, the last option is for general rooms.

Location Page

When you choose a specific room the icon appears on top and then you give it a specific location

Sidebar

This is how the sidebar looks. You will be able to log out from here or change windows.
